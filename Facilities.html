Facilities

To ensure a holistic education for all Marian learners, the school has made available the different facilities. The use followssome policies which are made clear at Orientation time. Strict observance of the policies trains the Marian learners to be orderly, considerate and responsible members of the school community and the society in general.

Library

Considered as the heart of any learning institution, the library is the center for all print resources of the school. Students and teachers alike go to the ND-RVMCC Library to avail of the latest acquisitions and the existing holdings needed in the particular subject of learnings. This is made update with an on-line source of the needed information. Also, the students’ reading interests are answered with the available leisure reading books and books related to their hobbies/ interest and even careers.

Audio-Visual Center

A special depository of the non-print, audio and visual learning materials is the Audio-Visual Center. Here, one finds learning aids that are electronic in nature, graphical representations of important lessons in the different subjects and technology – inspired in keeping with the signs of the times.

Laboratories

Computer Laboratory Fully equipped with hardware and software required by the school’s curriculum and supervised by professionals is the computer laboratory. It is fully air-conditioned and available for use by the students during their computer laboratory time. The laboratory serves as a venue for students to output their learnings during the lecture/ discussion time.

Internet Laboratory

Enhancing the computer-related experience of the students is the Internet Laboratory. This facility gives the students more free hand in using what they learn in their computer classes. The students use it as additional resource of their other subjects in addition to what they get from the resources of the Library.

Science Laboratory

The laboratories are so designed considering the nature of the Science classes. In the different laboratories, the students do experimentations that require them to set-up their experiments, observe, analyze data and make the necessary conclusions. The students use the facilities to try out their innovation of products applying principles learned in the classroom. The set-up of the laboratories promotes the desired values among the students.

Speech Laboratory

Fully equipped, the Speech Laboratory is another learning venue for the Marian students to be exposed to hands-on-learning. The students have the opportunities to listen to themselves, interact with one another and value the importance of speaking and listening as communication skills.

Cooking Laboratory

Used by all the students is the cooking laboratory that challenges their cooking prowess. Students do cooking tasks, cleaning/ restoring the venue after use, in addition to practicing what they learned in the lectures of the lesson. The students get to use modern gadgets as preparation for college life.

Sewing Laboratory

To reinforce the students’ learning of textile construction and actual sewing projects, the Sewing Laboratory has been provided for the use of the students. Sewing is the basic tool to enhance creative skills needed to contribute their share in the sustainable development of the country.

Gymnasium

Big enough to accommodate all the high school students, the gym has served many assemblies, socio-cultural activities, religious services of both Christians and non-Christians, departmental and institutional ceremonies as well as school and community activities.

Clinic

Manned by competent and dedicated personnel, the school clinic has been the place of relief for the students’ complaints of body pains. It has all the amenities to provide the needed comfort. The clinic also serves as a venue for medical and dental examinations of students.

Guidance Office

Strategically located is the guidance office that provides such services as Admission testing, Counseling, Individual Inventory Service, Career Counseling, and Placement and Follow-up. The office makes possible the students’ better understanding of everything about themselves, the world around them so they grow-up well-adjusted individuals.

Chapel

Accessible to all students, the chapel is one venue used for group and individual praying by both students, teachers and personnel. Masses, confessions and holy hours are held in the chapel that is conducive to religious practices.

Muslim Prayer Room

The room is a religious venue for all Moslems to practice their faith while they study in a Catholic school. The place is kept conducive for the purposes of the non-Christian students.

Canteen BookStore

Serving the students’ need for food and drink is the canteen. Meals and snacks are available and can be eaten in the canteen or in the open areas provided with tables and benches. School supplies are available in the Bookstore that facilitates the students’ purchase of learning materials.

Terrace II

Strategically located, the terrace is a multi-purpose venue for the department. It readily serves as bleachers during Intramurals and socio-cultural activities. It is also a venue for out-door classes and activities that require more elbow-room for students.
